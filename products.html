What you will get

You can download a property summary, which includes:

  • a 'location plan' - a map of the site with a red line round its boundary
  • a description of the property's planning constraints

We'll also introduce you to a land agent or developer who knows your area and can help you begin the process of developing and selling your land.

What will it cost?

Nothing! This service is totally free.
